Friends

"Now, I do hope you're not going to fight about me, because it's really not worth while."

In February, 2004, I asked my friends and acquantances to list their favourite top five movies of all time. The lists, as shown below, were wide as they were diverse. Very few titles appeared in more than one list. One, or two, had difficulty listing five - a reflection on perhaps the lack of really good movies out there. And for those that think they have chosen the perfect movie I have added a link to their mistakes page! Check out the lists.
